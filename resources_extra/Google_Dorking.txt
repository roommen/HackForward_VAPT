Google Dorking Cheatsheet:
*************************

1. allintext - searches for occurence all keywords given
2. intext - searches for the occurence of keywords all at once or one at a time
3. inurl - searches for a URL matching one of the keywords
4. allinurl - searches for a URL matching all the keywords in the query
5. intitle - searches for occurence of keywords in URL all or one
6. allintitle - searches for occurence of keywords all at a time
7. site - specifically searcg that particular site and lists all the results for that site
8. filetype - searches for a particular filetype mentioned in the query
9. link - searches for external links to pages
10. numrange - used to locate specific numbers in your searched
11. daterange - used to search within a particular date ranage
